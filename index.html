Welcome to the Cipher Treasure Hunt!

In this game, you will need to decode a series of ciphers using the Caesar cipher to find the hidden treasure. Each level becomes more difficult, and you must complete one level before advancing to the next.

How to Play:

  1. You will be given a ciphered word.
  2. The shift value to decode the cipher will be provided.
  3. Type in your decoded word and click "Submit".
  4. Correct answers move you to the next puzzle. If incorrect, try again!

Good luck!
